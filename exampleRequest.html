This pen pulls the top (40) color sets from Adobe Color CC (Kuler) using their API, renders them in a kind of nice way (even if the color swatches will not do as I would like them to). Click the "CSS" button and you will get a copy'n'paste ready CSS fragment ready to throw onto your website :-) 2016-01-31: Fixed error and improved CSS. 2017-08-25: Discovered issue as it seems that RSS feed is not available ATM - trying to find solution
